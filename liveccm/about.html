Our Story

Church History

1990

Founded by Bishop Robert James Kakande in Gayaza, Kabanyoro, Wakiso

1990s

Early growth and first branch plants in Uganda

2000s

Expansion to 10+ branches nationally

2025

Launch of updated online presence and digital ministry

Our Vision

To be a light to the nations, raising disciples who transform communities.

— Matthew 5:14

Our Mission

To serve through worship, education, and outreach.

— Micah 6:8

Statement of Faith

We believe in one true God — Father, Son, and Holy Spirit

We believe in the virgin birth, death, and resurrection of Jesus

We believe the Bible is the authoritative, inerrant Word of God

We believe in the person and Divinity of the Holy Spirit

We believe in the universal Church as the bride of Christ

We believe in the second advent of Jesus

We believe in the resurrection of the dead

We believe in the final judgment

We believe in a literal millennial rule of Jesus on earth
