What is Artificial Intelligence?

Artificial intelligence (AI) is the simulation of human intelligence in machines that are designed to learn, reason, and make decisions automatically.

AI enables computers to perform tasks such as understanding language, recognizing patterns, and solving problems without explicit programming.
---

How does Artificial Intelligence work?

Artificial intelligence works by using algorithms and data to train models that can recognize patterns and make predictions.

  • Data: AI systems learn from large datasets
  • Algorithms: Mathematical models process the data
  • Training: Models improve through repeated learning
---

Types of Artificial Intelligence

There are three main types of AI:

  • Narrow AI: Designed for specific tasks (e.g., chatbots, recommendation systems)
  • General AI: Human-level intelligence (still theoretical)
  • Super AI: More advanced than human intelligence (future concept)
---

Real-World Applications of AI

Artificial intelligence is used in many industries:

  • Healthcare (disease prediction and diagnosis)
  • Finance (fraud detection)
  • Social media (content recommendation)
  • Autonomous vehicles
---

Why Artificial Intelligence matters

Artificial intelligence is important because it improves efficiency, enables automation, and helps solve complex problems that are difficult for humans alone.
